- Changing locale:
  sudo steamos-readonly disable
  sudo pacman -S glibc  (glibc must be installed)
  sudo pacman -S nano
  sudo nano /etc/locale.gen (uncomment needed locales, don't comment en_US.UTF-8 UTF-8, it is needed)
  sudo locale-gen
